3. MEDLINE, PubMed, and PMC difference
    * PubMed (1996)
    * MEDLINE (1960) uses NLM controlled vocabulary (MeSH) to index citations.
    * MEDLINE is the largest subset of PubMed. 
    * PubMed Central (2000) - free archive for full-text biomedical and life science journal articles.
